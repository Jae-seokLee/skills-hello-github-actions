### Boltzmann equation
$$
\frac{\partial f}{\partial t}+\vec{v}\cdot\nabla_\vec{x} f+\frac{\vec{F}}{m}\cdot\nabla_\vec{v}f=\left(\frac{\partial f}{\partial t}\right)_\text{coll}
$$
